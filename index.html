01The Soil

Some things don’t need reinventing.

Long before fertiliser came in bags, Indian growers fed their fields with what the land gave back — the pressed cakes of neem, karanja and castor, returned to the soil after their oils were drawn.

It is one of the oldest habits in agriculture, and it has never stopped working. Meadows Agri Exports exists to carry that tradition outward: we work with established processors across India — mills that have pressed these seeds for generations — and bring their work up to the standard the world expects.

From a nursery’s potting bench to a plantation’s open rows, what we ship does one quiet, dependable thing — it feeds the soil, not just the season.
